It might be a good idea to keep away from enemy missiles. I advise you to move your mouse to do this cause the ship will follow the cursor. Press Ctrl to fire. But beware, your targeting computer can only guide one missile at a time ! The current highscore is 4495 on a PIII 700 MHz machine running NT4 with Opera 5 on full screen (1024x768).

If you're using Internet Explorer or Opera press F11 to play full screen.
